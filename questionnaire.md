# HackOnVibe — Project Questionnaire

**1. What does your application/service do?**

Undercut is an AI-powered social listening and automated lead acquisition platform that monitors competitor complaints on X (Twitter) and Instagram. It scrapes recent posts targeting specified competitors, filters out noise and irrelevant mentions using an AI classification gate (Gate 1), drafts contextual brand-aligned pitch replies (Gate 2) using your own product profile, differentiators, and chosen tone of voice, and routes the user directly to a pre-filled native Twitter intent link (or copies to clipboard for Instagram) so they can send human-approved pitches in one click.

**2. Who is the target audience?**

Indie hackers and solo founders looking to acquire their first adopters by presenting a better alternative to frustrated competitor users; SaaS growth teams seeking to scale organic acquisition channels; and bootstrap developers running on limited marketing budgets who want to acquire high-intent users directly.

**3. Which countries are the expected buyers of this service?**

Undercut targets a global market of creators and startups. Key markets include the United States and Europe (high density of startups, SaaS founders, and growth hackers), and Southeast Asia — especially Indonesia and Singapore — a rapidly expanding community of tech builders and indie hackers looking for cost-effective organic growth.

**4. Who are your competitors?**

Traditional social listening suites (e.g., Brand24, Mention, Sprout Social) which are powerful but expensive, complex to configure, and lack automated product-aware AI pitch drafting; and Twitter-only growth tools (e.g., TweetHunter) which are great for scheduling and general engagement but not specialized in intercepting and pitching competitor complaints across multiple platforms.

**5. What is your advantage?**

Two-Gate AI Filtering lowers cost and noise by instantly removing junk posts (Gate 1) before generating response pitches (Gate 2). An anti-bot philosophy generates drafts and lets the user review and reply with a single click via native web intents — maintaining account safety and trust instead of risking bans from bot spamming. A prepaid credit system offers flexible Stripe top-ups with tiered bonuses paired with free credits that reset weekly, and the UI is mobile-responsive so founders can monitor leads and pitch alternatives on the go.
